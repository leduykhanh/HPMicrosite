Smart, Curious, Hands on.

Lance Snider working in the Bonneville Salt Flats, Utah

Smart, Curious, Hands on.

Those are the qualities we look for in our team. Other words that might describe us, and hopefully you too, include: friendly, enthusiastic, inventive, persistent, funny (some more than others), creative, geeky and proficient. We also wanted to say profligate because it sounds good, but we looked it up and it really isn’t us.

Envato is headquartered in Melbourne, Australia, but a sizable number of Envato staff work remotely and autonomously from home offices, coffee shops, or anywhere with an internet connection. We have team members in Canada, USA, UK, Malaysia, Romania, Latvia, France, Spain, Italy, Germany, Sweden, The Netherlands, India, and Belgium; from urban centres to the remotest of spots.

When We Say Remote We Mean Remote!

Working at Envato is about being a part of our internal community. We all work hard to make Envato both a successful company, and a fun, friendly and happy workplace. From catered lunchtime talks, a communal garden, online letterpress tournaments, a lively Yammer channel and the odd ‘party pants’ day in the office, working at Envato is about doing a great job and having a great place to do it at.

  • The community is our backbone and we always try to make decisions with their best interests in mind. It is critical to us that we have a win-win approach to work, and that we get involved with and talk to the community.

03

A Fun, Friendly HQ

Ext to our well worn, and slightly wonky pool table.

A Fun, Friendly HQ

When you work in front of a screen all day, it’s good to get up and about. That’s why Envato HQ is home to a slightly off-centre pool table, a retro arcade table, a massage chair and a culture of stand-up meetings, bean bag chats and the occasional developer dressed in a giant box pretending to be a robot. You also stand a good chance of getting photo bombed.

  • The community is our backbone and we always try to make decisions with their best interests in mind. It is critical to us that we have a win-win approach to work, and that we get involved with and talk to the community.

  • Everyone deserves an equal chance and a fair opportunity. At work, on our sites, and in life we strive to be fair and consistent in everything we do.
