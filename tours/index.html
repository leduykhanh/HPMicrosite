Solutions for
the New Style of Business

(TECHNOLOGY TOUR)

We are living in an ultra-connected, digital world. Technology is impacting every part of how we live and work.  Technology has disrupted governments and every industry. To thrive in this new world, businesses and governments will have to continuously alter its DNA.

Solutions for the New Style of Business is HPE’s point of view on how the world is changing with the rise in the new wave of technology which comprises social media, mobility, big data & cloud. This changes the way technology is built, delivered & consumed.

Solutions for the New Style of Business promises simplicity, greater agility, speed & lower costs. HPE’s heritage of innovations as well as our DNA of supporting open systems and partnering is well positioned to help customers address the challenges and opportunities in this new market paradigm.


Learn how HP’s innovations in convergence, cloud and software defined data center can deliver comprehensive solutions which customers need to solve their toughest business problems and not just a set of disparate IT assets.

HP Converged System

(TECHNOLOGY TOUR)

Applications matter. And they matter even more in the New Style of Business. HPE ConvergedSystem is designed as an infrastructure solution for the New Style of Business which can quickly harness the power of the application workload and accelerate your business results.

HPE ConvergedSystems are purpose-built systems optimized for specific workloads and for fast deployment & quick time- to-value. ConvergedSystems support a range of common workloads such as virtualization, SAP HANA, Microsoft Analytics Platform, CRM & ERP applications.

Learn how ConvergedSystems can be the building block for your Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) vision which can deliver faster time-to-value, reduce costs and risks and increase your business’s competitive advantage.

Hyper-Connected Workplace


(TECHNOLOGY TOUR)

Evolving business demands are reshaping the modern workplace. Increasingly a diverse geographically distributed workforce working in an organization need different desktop experience to accomplish their tasks. Application and desktop virtualization helps address these demands by allowing your organization to centralize apps and desktops in the datacenter and securely deliver them to users on demand, to the right device, anywhere.

Learn about HPE’s range of VDI, HDI and eVDI solutions for the hyper-connected modern workplace delivering the right desktop compute for the typical task workers to the knowledge workers and finally to the workstation-class users.
