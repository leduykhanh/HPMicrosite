About CEC

Bringing HPE Innovation to our APJ Customers

The Customer Engagement Centre (CEC) located in Singapore, provides a facility for customers to gain insights and explore the business value of HPE entire products & solutions portfolio. CEC provides a forum to :

  • Identify customer priorities & interests
  • Discuss HPE’s technology direction and key strategies
  • Showcase relevant technology solutions Meet with HPE senior executives and subject matter experts
  • Build closer business relationships Collaborate with strategic alliances & partners
Maximizing Engagement of Partner Solutions and Applications  

With many exciting tours and unique scenarios depicted, the CEC also provides Partners with many opportunities to engage with high-level customers through demonstrations of their applications and solutions. Contact Beng Huat to check on the availability.

A Conducive and Lively Meeting Area

The CEC has 2 meeting areas that come fully equipped with projectors, large screen panels, audio capabilities, presentation clickers and comfortable seating for up to 12 pax. With the added capability to present solution demonstrations, CEC is the perfect place to host internal or external meetings. Contact Beng Huat to check on the availability.

WHERE IS CEC?

The CEC Singapore has hosted nearly 250 customer and partner accounts in FY2015, touching over a reported $250 M in potential HPE revenue.The center staff prides itself on the ability to tailor briefings to the customers‘ needs, working with account teams for weeks prior to the briefing to ensure the goal of the briefing is clear to all involved
