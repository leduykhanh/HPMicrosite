About Demo & Workshop

Workshops can be an effective platform to align business & IT, understanding the current situation as well as future aspirations and developing a high-level action plan with a holistic approach to IT infrastructure transformation with the aim of creating value & positive business outcome for the company.

Demos can be an effective and powerful tool to showcase HPE's technology proof points to the customers.

Our comprehensive demo & workshop portfolio span across 4 Transformation Areas to cover the entire spectrum of HPE products and technologies:

With many exciting tours and unique scenarios depicted, the CEC also provides Partners with many opportunities to engage with high-level customers through demonstrations of their applications and solutions. Contact Beng Huat to check on the availability.

The CEC has 2 meeting areas that come fully equipped with projectors, large screen panels, audio capabilities, presentation clickers and comfortable seating for up to 12 pax. With the added capability to present solution demonstrations, CEC is the perfect place to host internal or external meetings. Contact Beng Huat to check on the availability.

Data Center Interconnect (EVI/MDC)

Showcase the following features :

  • Ethernet Virtual Interconnect (EVI) which is a HPE Virtual Application Network innovation providing a Layer 2 extension across the data center to simplify the interconnectivity of geographically dispersed data centers
  • Multi-tenant Device Context (MDC) is another innovative Data Center virtualization solution that enables multi-tenancy, giving you the ability to virtualize a physical switch into multiple topical devices, with each logical switch having its own tenants
