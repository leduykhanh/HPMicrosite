Understand Customer

by leveraging the Customer Engagement Centre

  • At this sales cycle stage, it is important to do the following:
    • Identify the business drivers that are impacting the customer's business
    • Identify customer business initiatives and approaches they are puting in the place to deal with or react to the business drivers
    • How are mega trends such as cloud, big data, security, social media & mobility impacting the customer's business
    • What are the customer obstacles that impact the most successful implementation of business initiatives?
    • What is the impact if they do not address these obstacles?
  • Propose customer to visit the Customer Engagement Centre

Validate Opportunity

to get started

  • At this sales cycle stage, it is important to do the following:
    • Describe how the product/solution features address customer requirements and business challenges
    • Identify discovery questions that will enable you understand the customer problems
    • Describe case scenarios to include realistic and attainable goals provided by use
    • Describe how HP provides a competitive advantage
  • Propose customer to visit the Customer Engagement Centre
  • Propose discovery workshop to customer
  • Propose meetings with subject matter experts to customer

Qualify Opportunity

  • At this sales cycle stage, it is important to do the following:
    • Identify key benefits and business value relevant to the customer
    • How do the features address the requirements & address the problems?
      • Improve their business performance eg. increase revenue, decrease cost, minimize risks.
    • Why is this important to customers and what does it mean for them?
    • How much of customer IT budget is to be spent on addressing the requirements?
    • Who are the key decision makers to move this initiative forward?
    • What are the timelines for this initiative?
  • Submit solution proposal to customer

Develop & Propose

  • At this sales cycle stage, it is important to do the following:
    • Determine resource requirements
    • Evaluate and select partners
    • Establish opportunity bid team
    • Define solution based on customer requirements
    • Build value proposition
    • Develop SOW for POC & benchmarking (if required)
    • Meet with customer to review proposed solution
  • Issue final proposal to customer

Negotiate & Close

  • At this sales cycle stage, it is important to confirm:
    • Can our solution solve the customer problems?
    • Have we demonstrated proof of ROI?
    • Are there any technical concerns on the solution?
    • Is there real interest to purchase?
  • Before going into a price negotiation stage

Win & Deploy

  • At this sales cycle stage, it is important to do the following:
    • Finalize contract negotiations and secure signatures
    • Finalize support plan of partners and services
    • Establish customer deployment conditions of satisfaction with partners
    • Assess deployment readiness
  • Conduct post-deployment follow-up
  • Identify additional opportunities
  • Transition deployment ownership
  • Follow-up with customer on success & reference
